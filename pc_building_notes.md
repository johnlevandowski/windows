PC Building Notes
=================

Thermalright cpu cooler - screw one side so it just barely catches before screwing opposite side, it also requires significant downforce on cooler because of the high tension springs  
Metallic/Plastic smell from new components is from off-gasing - if temps are fine under load, it should be good  
Take screen shot of motherboard internal connectors  
Take screen shot of motherboard front panel connectors  

First Boot Notes
================

When booting PC for first time it can take a while to POST.  On gigagbyte motherboards it can take 5-10 minutes while memory trains.  Just leave pc on and wait.  
If PC doesn't boot with monitor connected to GPU, try using a different port on the GPU or connecting to integrated graphics (if available).
